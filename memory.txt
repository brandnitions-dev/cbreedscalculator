### Chemistry: Powder vs Oil-form C (oil absorption tax)
- **Dry powders absorb oil from tallow.** Each C ingredient has `oilAbsorb` (g oil per g powder):
  - Sugar: 0.08, Baking soda: 0.12, Walnut/shell: 0.15, Coffee grounds: 0.30, Ginger powder: 0.35, Oat flour: 0.40, Clay: 0.50
- **Oil-form C** (infused oil, paste) displaces carrier oil 1:1 ‚Äî `oilAbsorb: 0`.
- `fbCalcFormula()` computes `oilTax = sum(cRow.pct √ó oilAbsorb)` and adds it to tallow.
- Lab Worksheet uses fixed `LAB_AVG_OIL_ABSORB = 0.25` (midrange powder) since it doesn't track individual C items.
- `calcFromTallowBal()` includes `cPctVal * 0.25` in `talFrac` so tallow‚Üíjojoba back-solve is stable.
- Form selector UI shows: powder ‚Üí "(dry ‚Äî absorbs X% its weight in oil from tallow)", oil ‚Üí "(oil phase ‚Äî displaces carrier 1:1)".
- Each C ingredient has `phase: "dry"` or `phase: "oil"` (multi-form items carry phase in `formMeta`).

### Chemistry: Potency-aware maxPct caps
- Each C ingredient has `maxPct` (max safe % of total batch for that single ingredient).
- Multi-form items carry `maxPct` in `formMeta` ‚Äî powder forms get stricter caps than oil forms.
- Caps: Sugar 15%, Oat flour 12%, Coffee powder 12% / oil 15%, Clay powder 8% / oil 12%, Baking soda 8%, Walnut/shell 6%, **Ginger powder 5%** / oil 12%.
- `fbCalcEffectiveMaxC()` computes weighted-average max C% across the pool ‚Äî e.g., ginger powder (5%) + sugar (15%) at equal weights ‚Üí ~10% effective max.
- `fbCalcFormula()` clamps `cPct` to the effective max. `fbUpdate()` dynamically sets the slider's `max` attribute and shows "capped by potent ingredient" note.
- `fbUpdateSynergies()` warns which ingredient is limiting and suggests switching to oil-form for higher safe range.
- Lab Worksheet `labCPct()` also clamps via `fbCalcEffectiveMaxC()`. `onLabCStrengthChange()` clamps the lab slider.
- Helper: `fbGetCMaxPct(row)` resolves maxPct for a row (formMeta first, then top-level, fallback 0.15).

### Chemistry: Structural & therapeutic warnings
- **No beeswax** ‚Äî scrub has no structural hold at room temp; loose oil-paste. Warned in synergies + production steps.
- **No A + B** ‚Äî 100% tallow base with no therapeutic carriers. Warned in synergies.
- **High total oil absorption (>3%)** ‚Äî scrub may feel dry/stiff. Warned with suggestion to use oil-form or add carrier A.
- **Mixed dry + oil C** ‚Äî oil-form dissolves into base, dry sits suspended. Fold dry in last.

## Eye Revival Serum Formula Audit (2026-04-29)
### Problem
Original eye serum formula (eyes mode in balm builder) had critical flaws:
- No emulsifier ‚Äî 60% water + 28% oil will separate immediately
- No preservative ‚Äî water-based formula at severe microbial risk near eyes
- Oil phase 28% too heavy for eye area (standard: 5‚Äì15%)
- Vitamin E at 2% overdosed (should be 0.5% antioxidant, not preservative)
- Licorice root at 3.2% above safe range (max 2%)
- No pH specification (actives are pH-sensitive)
- Wrong product classification ("Tallow Balm" ‚Äî it contains no tallow)

### Corrected Formula (v2)
- Water phase 60.5%: Aloe 35%, Green tea hydrosol 20%, Glycerin 5.5%
- Oil phase ~14%: Squalane 8%, Jojoba 4%, Carrier A 9.4% (prickly pear + rosehip shared)
- Emulsifier 6%: Olivem 1000 (Cetearyl Olivate + Sorbitan Olivate) ‚Äî EcoCert
- Preservative 1.5%: Geogard Ultra (Gluconolactone + Sodium Benzoate)
- Chelator 0.1%: Phytic acid (prevents rosehip oxidation)
- Vitamin E 0.5%: Oil-phase antioxidant, add before emulsification
- Actives 8%: Caffeine ~2.9%, Gotu kola ~2%, Licorice root ‚â§2%, Bakuchiol ~1%
- pH target: 5.0‚Äì5.5
- Shelf life: 6 months (3M PAO)

### Default Pool Changes (formula-builder.tsx)
- Carrier A: pricklypear weight 6‚Üí2, rosehip weight 4‚Üí2
- Active B: caffeine_extract weight 2‚Üí3, tremella‚Üígotukola (weight 2), licorice_root weight 2 (unchanged), bakuchiol weight 1 (unchanged)
