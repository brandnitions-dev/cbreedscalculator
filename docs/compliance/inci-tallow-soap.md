# INCI Label Draft — Cold-Process Tallow Soap

> Rinse-off cleansing bar — saponified oils  
> Default: 55% Tallow, 25% Olive, 10% Coconut, 5% Castor, 3% Beeswax, 2% Shea

---

## Default Recipe INCI

### English

```
INGREDIENTS: Sodium Tallowate, Sodium Olivate, Aqua (Water), 
Sodium Cocoate, Sodium Castorate, Glycerin*, Sodium Beeswax**, 
Sodium Shea Butterate, [Essential Oil(s) — see below].

May contain: [Allergens based on EO selection].

*Naturally produced during saponification.
**Cera Alba saponified with sodium hydroxide.
```

> **Note**: In cold-process soap, the INCI lists the **saponified** forms (Sodium ___ate) rather than the raw oils, because the oils have been chemically transformed. The water and glycerin are byproducts of saponification.

## Superfat Note

> Soap with X% superfat means X% of the oils remain **unsaponified** in the final bar. These provide extra moisturizing but are listed as their raw oil INCI names (e.g., Olea Europaea Fruit Oil) if above 1%.

For a 5% superfat soap, add at the end of INCI:

```
..., Olea Europaea Fruit Oil*, Cocos Nucifera Oil*.
*Unsaponified (superfat).
```

## Cure & pH

| Metric | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Cure time | 4–6 weeks minimum |
| Final pH | 8–10 (test with pH strips before selling) |
| Zap test | Touch bar to tongue tip — no zap = safe |
