# INCI Label Draft — Exfoliator / Treatment Oil

> Anhydrous carrier oil blend — leave-on treatment. No tallow.

---

## BHA Penetrating Oil (Home Use)

```
INGREDIENTS: Simmondsia Chinensis Seed Oil, Rosa Canina Fruit Oil, 
Salix Alba Bark Extract, Boswellia Carterii Oil, 
Gaultheria Procumbens Leaf Oil, Melaleuca Alternifolia Leaf Oil.

May contain: Limonene, Linalool, Methyl Salicylate.
```

## Safety Warnings (wintergreen products)

```
⚠️ Contains methyl salicylate (wintergreen oil).
⚠️ Do not use on children under 12.
⚠️ Avoid during pregnancy/breastfeeding without medical advice.
⚠️ Apply SPF 30+ after use — BHA increases sun sensitivity.
⚠️ For external use only. Patch test before first use.
```
