# INCI Label Draft — Eye Revival Serum

> **Product classification:** Eye Serum / Leave-on emulsion (O/W)  
> **NOT a tallow balm** — this is a water-oil emulsion containing no tallow

## Manufacturing Process

### Equipment Required
- Digital scale (0.01g precision)
- Calibrated pH meter
- Thermometer
- High-shear mixer or immersion blender
- Heat-safe beakers (2× for phases)
- Sanitized containers

### Process Steps

1. **Sanitize** all equipment and workspace per GMP manual
2. **Water phase (A):** Combine aloe vera juice, green tea hydrosol, and glycerin. Heat to 70°C. Add caffeine and water-soluble actives (gotu kola, licorice root). Stir until fully dissolved.
3. **Oil phase (B):** Combine squalane, jojoba, prickly pear, rosehip, and vitamin E (0.5%). Add Olivem 1000 emulsifier. Heat to 70°C until emulsifier is fully melted.
4. **Emulsification:** Pour oil phase INTO water phase slowly while using high-shear mixer. Blend for 3–5 minutes until uniform, creamy emulsion forms.
5. **Cool-down phase:** Continue mixing at low speed. When temperature drops below 40°C, add:
   - Bakuchiol (oil-soluble active)
   - Geogard Ultra preservative
   - Phytic acid chelator
6. **pH check:** Test with calibrated pH meter. Target **5.0–5.5**.
   - Too high → add citric acid solution drop by drop
   - Too low → add sodium hydroxide solution drop by drop
7. **Fill and label**

### Critical Quality Checks

| Check | Target | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| pH | 5.0–5.5 | Every batch |
| Visual separation | None visible after 24hr | Every batch |
| Preservative efficacy (PET) | Pass USP <51> or ISO 11930 | First batch + annually |
| Stability test | No separation, odor change, or pH drift >0.5 | 1wk, 1mo, 3mo, 6mo |
| Accelerated stability (40°C/75% RH) | Stable 3 months | Before market launch |

## Regulatory Notes

- **Product classification:** Cosmetic product under EC 1223/2009
- **PIF required:** Product Information File must classify as "Eye Serum / Leave-on emulsion" — NOT "Tallow Balm"
- **CPNP notification:** Required before placing on EU market
- **Safety assessment:** Must include preservative efficacy test results
- **Claims:** Do NOT make retinol-alternative claims with bakuchiol below 2%. Acceptable: "contains bakuchiol" or "with plant-based skin renewal support"
- **Allergen disclosure:** No EU 26 allergens present (no essential oils in formula)
